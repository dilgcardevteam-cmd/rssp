Need a clear workflow for HR Division actions?

This manual is for the hr_division role.

Admin Dashboard

Steps:

  1. Open dashboard.
  2. Review pending activities.
  3. Continue to required module.

Vacancies Management (COS)

Steps:

  1. Open Vacancies Management.
  2. Add or edit COS vacancies.
  3. Save and verify listing.

Applicants Management

Steps:

  1. Open Applicants Management.
  2. Filter by vacancy and status.
  3. Review and update applicant decisions.

Exam Management

Steps:

  1. Open Exam Management.
  2. Configure exam details for assigned vacancy.
  3. Monitor attendance and status updates.

Positions Utility

Steps:

  1. Open Positions utility.
  2. Review available positions and mappings.
  3. Save updates if permitted.
